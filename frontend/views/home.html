What is eQueue?

Have you been bored of waiting in endless queues? eQueue is a new app promises to help you to save daily a lot of time

How it works?

If you are a user client, you can subscribe on queue of a specific company, apply for a queue number and be notified about the current status of queue and the remaining time for your turn on queue. If you are a business client, you can register your company and take advantages of the app to serve your clients more effectively.

Actions

REGISTER/ LOGIN/ LOGOUT/ MY QUEUES/ APPLY FOR A QUEUE NUMBER/ UPDATE QUEUE STATUS
